Overview

T2AV-Bench is designed to stress-test multimodal models beyond single-score evaluation. We organize benchmark tasks around orthogonal dimensions and aggregate them into a transparent, diagnosis-friendly evaluation protocol.

Instead of only asking whether outputs look good, we ask where and why systems fail: semantic drift, audio mismatch, temporal instability, or content-level inconsistency.

Evaluation Dimensions

Semantic Alignment

Does generated content faithfully satisfy textual intent and key entities?

Audiovisual Consistency

Are sound events synchronized and semantically coherent with visual content?

Temporal Stability

How stable are motion, identity, and scene structure across frames?

Perceptual Quality

Does the output maintain sharpness, naturalness, and low artifact rates?

Dataset and Protocol

  • Large-scale prompts covering actions, scenes, and audio conditions.
  • Automatic and model-based evaluators with consistent preprocessing.
  • Detailed JSON outputs for reproducible diagnostics and ablation studies.
